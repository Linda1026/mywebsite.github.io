Plot

SUMMARY

  • "Zootopia" is a movie about a bunny named Judy Hopps who dreams of becoming a police officer in the big city of Zootopia, where all kinds of animals live together. When she finally becomes an officer, she discovers that it's tough being the first bunny on a police force made up of big, tough animals. She's determined to prove herself and jumps at the chance to solve a mysterious case.
  • Judy teams up with a sly fox named Nick Wilde, even though foxes and bunnies aren't supposed to get along. At first, they don't trust each other, but as they work together, they become friends. They discover that the case is bigger than they thought—it involves missing animals and a secret plot that could hurt the peace in Zootopia.
  • Using their brains and bravery, Judy and Nick solve the mystery and save the day, showing that no matter what kind of animal you are, you can be anything you want to be. They also learn that it's important not to judge others based on what they look like or where they come from.
